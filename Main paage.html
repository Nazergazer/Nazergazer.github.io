Information about the website

What is Trendly Stats?

Trendly Stats is a website that uses simple data analysis and comparisons to help people explore popular YouTube topics. It provides clear graphics, insights, and information to help you explore YouTube, whether you're looking for content ideas or just curious about trends.

What is YouTube?
YouTube is an American video-sharing platform where you can upload, watch, and share videos owned by Google. As of January 2025, the platform has 2.53 billion monthly active users worldwide. The enormous number of videos available on YouTube is one of the many factors contributing to its popularity. YouTube receives 100 hours of video uploads every minute on average, so there's always something new to watch!
